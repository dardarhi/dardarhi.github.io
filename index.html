Compatability

    Some people know astrology for its' compatibility aspect. Some astrological signs are said to go better with each other than others. Every sign has an opposite sign, which is sometimes deemed as their best match.
    Essentially, their opposite sign are very similar and very different in the sense that they make up what the other may lack. They possess every quality needed to create perfect union and completion.
    Of course, don't not engage in relationships with someone if you're sign isn't compatible. Astrology is vague and broad for a reason. But some other signs go better if they are in the same element. Like fire and fire (Aries and Sagittarius) or (Geminis and Libras) both are air signs.

Personality

    According to astrology, the position of celestial bodies, which are both planets and other stars, of one's birth time, can determine certain personality traits and likelihoods.
    Let's take the sign of Leo, they are said to be confident, extremely loving, and are natural leaders. There are a lot of notions and assumptions that most Leos are 'narcissistic' and egotistical. This may be true for most Leos, but it doesn't mean all of them brag about themselves and want everything to be about them all the time.
    Some may just love to talk about themselves or their problems, but don't like to socialize and be free in front of everyone. Some tendencies do line up with most signs, like this one, but usually not in the same exact way.

Did you know that the signs that tend to be the most compatabile are their exact opposites.

      Aries- Libra
      Taurus- Scorpio
      Gemini- Sagitarius
      Cancer- Capricorn
      Aquarius- Leo
      Pisces- Virgo

And the have a lot of similarities to each other, which is kind of ironic. Usually opposites are quite different, and this usually feeds into the idea that opposites attract, but sometimes they can be quite similar. An example of this is with Aqurius and Leos, both tend to be into themselves and know how to priortize themselves. However, Aquarians are more subtle, and tend to be one of the smarter and more innovative signs.
